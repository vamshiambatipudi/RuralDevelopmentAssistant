🌾 Rural Development Assistant (RDA)
Project Overview:
Rural Development Assistant (RDA) is an AI-Powered Web Application designed to support rural communities by providing intelligent services such as Crop Recommendation, Government Schemes, Job Opportunities, Telemedicine, and Weather Forecasting.
The system helps farmers, youth, and rural citizens make data-driven decisions for better livelihood and development.

Objectives
* Provide AI-based crop recommendation
* Suggest eligible government schemes
* Provide job opportunities for rural youth
* Offer telemedicine chatbot assistance
* Provide real-time weather forecasting
* Improve rural digital accessibility

* Soil type analysis

💡 Conclusion
Rural Development Assistant provides AI-based smart solutions to empower rural communities and promote digital rural transformation.
